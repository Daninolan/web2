AGE POLICY
You must be aged 18 years or over to attend Electric Picnic Music and Arts Festival, with the exception of children aged 12 and under. As in previous years, 13-17 year olds are not permitted to attend the event even when accompanied by an adult. It is not acceptable to turn up to the festival with children aged between 13-17 and anyone who is of this age will not gain admission to the festival.

Children aged 12 years and under must be accompanied by a ticket holding adult throughout the weekend, limited to a maximum of two children per adult. Children aged 12 years and under do not require a ticket but they must be registered before arriving at the festival. If you are bringing your child and feel they look older than 12 years old please bring ID to back this up (colour photocopies will suffice).

Those wishing to stay in the Family Campsite must be accompanied by children aged 12 years and under.

Additional information

ID
We have a Challenge 25 Policy on site and the following are accepted as ID for the bars and cigarettes.
The accepted forms of ID are

  • Passport
  • Drivers Licence
  • Garda Age Cards
Please note that ID checks will also be in place on entry to the festival. If you are bringing your child and feel they look older than 12 years old please bring ID to back this up (colour photocopies will suffice). It is also not acceptable to turn up to the festival with children aged between 13-17 years old. Children in this age range will not gain free admission to the festival or otherwise.
We reserve the right to refuse admission without a refund if you cannot prove your age.

THINGS YOU CAN AND CAN'T BRING
You may be body and/or bag searched at the entrance, onsite or on leaving. Any item(s), which may reasonably be considered for use as a weapon, or which may cause danger or disruption to any other persons at the event, will be confiscated. Persons suspected of carrying items that may be used in an offensive or dangerous manner, or carrying out illegal activities within the arena or other parts of the site may be searched.
